SMA is a selected archive of works, projects and researches (vaguely) organized in thematic arrays. Here you can find mainly 3d modeling, visualization and graphic design, together with scripting experiments and web development.

The common ground is the design anf the display of information, whether as solid matter (buildings and objects), fluid layouts (graphic and web design) or dynamic systems (crowds behaviors in urban environments). The design of this page is based on and paper.js library. And yes I believe everything is a remix.

Please have a look around and feel free to contact me if you are interested in a collaboration.
